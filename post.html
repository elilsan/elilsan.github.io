My visit to Vancouver, Canada

It was by far one of the best days of my life

Posted by elilsan on January 19, 2024

It has almost been a year since I have last been to Vancouver and I am reflecting on my experience as I write this.

Although, it was not my first time visitng, it was still just as pretty and chilly as I remembered.

While I was there, I was again reminded of how clean the city was along with its majestic mountains in the background. Everything about its landscape was so beautiful and I took my time just living in the moment.

Although I personally would probably never live there, visiting the city is just as satisfying. I think that its a good weekend getaway place, but the unaffordability is a real issue. I hope that the economy improves there for the sake of its residents.
